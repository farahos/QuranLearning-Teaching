2.  User  Personas  
Persona  Goals  &  Needs  
Student  Wants  to  find  a  compatible  Quran  teacher,  view  their  teaching  style  via  video,  check  student  reviews,  and  pay  securely.  
Teacher  Wants  to  list  courses,  showcase  expertise  through  an  intro  video,  manage  earnings  in  a  digital  wallet,  and  communicate  with  students.  
3.  Functional  Requirements  
3.1  Teacher  Profiles  &  Course  Listings  
●  Course  Management:  Teachers  can  create  listings  featuring  the  Course  Name,  detailed  
description,
 
and
 
the
 
specific
 
Fee/Price.
 ●  Introduction  Video:  Integration  of  a  high-performance  video  player.  ○  Technical  Note:  Use  Mux  or  Cloudinary  for  adaptive  bitrate  streaming  to  ensure  
zero
 
lag
 
for
 
students
 
globally.
 ●  WhatsApp  Integration:  A  "Contact  on  WhatsApp"  CTA  that  triggers  a  pre-filled  message  
to
 
the
 
teacher's
 
registered
 
number.

3.2  Student  Feedback  System  
●  Star  Ratings:  A  1-5  star  rating  system  for  students  to  rate  their  learning  experience.  ●  Written  Reviews:  A  text  area  for  students  to  leave  qualitative  feedback.  ●  Trust  Metrics:  Profiles  must  display  an  average  rating  and  total  number  of  reviews  to  
establish
 
social
 
proof.
 
3.3  In-App  Wallet  &  Financials  
●  Wallet  Dashboard:  A  real-time  view  of  current  balance,  pending  funds,  and  total  
earnings.
 ●  Security  (KYC):  A  mandatory  Know  Your  Customer  module.  Teachers  must  upload  
government
 
ID
 
and
 
verify
 
their
 
identity
 
before
 
the
 
"Withdraw"
 
function
 
is
 
unlocked.
 ●  Transactions:  Ability  to  "Deposit"  funds  for  students  and  "Withdraw"  to  local  bank  
accounts
 
or
 
mobile
 
money
 
for
 
teachers.
 
4.  User  Journey  Map  
1.  Discovery:  Student  browses  the  marketplace  and  filters  by  course  type.  2.  Evaluation:  Student  watches  the  teacher's  Intro  Video  and  reads  previous  student  
ratings/reviews.
 3.  Inquiry:  Student  clicks  the  WhatsApp  button  to  discuss  scheduling  or  specific  needs.  4.  Payment:  Student  pays  the  course  fee  through  the  app;  funds  are  recorded  in  the  
system.
 5.  Onboarding:  Teacher  provides  the  link  to  the  Private  Telegram  Channel  where  the  full  
course
 
material
 
and
 
live
 
sessions
 
reside.
 
5.  Technical  Recommendations  
Feature  Recommended  Integration  
Video  Hosting  Mux  Video  API  (for  fast,  optimized  playback)  
Communication  WhatsApp  Business  API  /  Telegram  Bot  API  
Payments  Stripe,  PayPal,  or  local  Mobile  Money  APIs  
Identity  Verification  Jumio  or  Onfido  (for  automated  KYC)
